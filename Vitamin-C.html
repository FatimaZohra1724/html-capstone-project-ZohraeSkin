🌞 About Vitamin C – Powered by ZohraeSkin

It also known as Ascorbic Acid..

Vitamin C is a brightening and antioxidant-rich skincare ingredient. It helps to boost collagen, reduce dark spots, and protect your skin from environmental damage like pollution and UV rays.

It’s a favorite for achieving radiant, glowing, and even-toned skin.

🍊 Key Features of Vitamin-C

    ✅ Brightens dull skin – Adds natural radiance and glow

    ✅ Boosts collagen – Improves skin firmness and elasticity

    ✅ Fades dark spots – Helps lighten hyperpigmentation and acne marks

    ✅ Fights free radicals – Protects from sun damage and pollution

    ✅ Evens skin tone – Helps reduce redness and blotchiness

    ✅ Smooths fine lines – Supports youthful, healthy skin

🧴 Who Can Use Vitamin-C ?

  1. Normal Skin – Enhances glow and brightness.
  2. Oily Skin – Brightens without clogging pores.
  3. Dry Skin – Works well under moisturizer for hydration.
  4. Combination Skin – Targets multiple skin issues at once.
  5. Aging Skin – Reduces fine lines and uneven skin tone.

💬 Skin Problems Treated by Vitamin-C :-

#Most Common Skin Concern
  1. Dullness – Revives tired and lackluster skin
  2. Dark Spots – Visibly fades acne marks and sunspots
  3. Uneven Skin Tone – Promotes clear and smooth complexion
  4. Early Signs of Aging – Smooths wrinkles and fine lines
  5. Sun Damage – Reduces oxidative stress caused by UV rays
  6. Rough Texture – Helps improve skin smoothness

💡 Use Vitamin C serum in the morning routine for best protection and glow. Apply after cleansing and before sunscreen or moisturizer.
